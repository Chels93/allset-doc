Documentation Essentials

Key concepts and buzzwords every physician should master for accurate ICD-10 coding.

Clinical Indicators

  • Current medical conditions + status
  • All abnormal findings
  • Clinical evidence of diagnosis
  • Risk factors, signs, symptoms
  • Test results documentation

Complexity Factors

  • Complicated vs. uncomplicated
  • Intractable vs. tractable
  • Function/life threatening levels
  • Major, minor, self-limited
  • Medical decision-making complexity

Timing & Intent

  • ICD-10-CM intent: Accidental, assault
  • Intentional self-harm, undetermined
  • Onset prior to/during/after procedure
  • 7th character: initial, subsequent, sequela
  • Trimester 1,2,3, childbirth, puerperium

Special Circumstances

  • Personal history of (PHO)
  • Present on admission (POA)
  • External causes & SDoH
  • EtOH, medications, tobacco, transplant
  • DNR, poor prognosis, de-escalation
